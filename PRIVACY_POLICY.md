# Boxerium Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 21 May 2026_

This is the privacy policy for **Boxerium** (the "App"), a boxing round-timer
application for iOS developed by Ethan Brown ("we", "us"). This policy explains
what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

## Information we collect

**Information stored only on your device.** Boxerium stores the following data
locally on your device (in iOS's standard app preferences). This data never
leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party:

- Workouts you create (round counts, durations, combo lists, favorites)
- Your in-app settings (selected text-to-speech voice, callout preferences)
- Your purchase status for the "Remove Ads" subscription

**Information collected by third parties.** Boxerium uses the following
third-party services that may collect information about your use of the App:

- **Google AdMob** — used to display banner advertisements when you have not
  subscribed to "Remove Ads". AdMob may collect device identifiers (including
  the Apple IDFA, if you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency
  prompt), IP address, approximate location derived from IP, and ad
  interaction data. See Google's privacy disclosures at
  <https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites> and
  <https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543>.
- **Apple (StoreKit / App Store)** — used to process the "Remove Ads"
  subscription. Apple receives standard purchase metadata; we receive an
  anonymous receipt confirming the subscription is active. We never receive
  your name, email, payment details, or Apple ID. See Apple's
  privacy policy at <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>.

We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal account information,
contacts, photos, microphone audio, location, or any other personal data
beyond what is described above.

## App Tracking Transparency

On first launch on iOS 14.5 and later, Boxerium will ask you whether you allow
us (via AdMob) to track your activity across other companies' apps and
websites. You may choose **Allow** or **Ask App Not to Track** — both options
let you use the App fully. If you allow tracking, AdMob will use your IDFA to
serve more relevant ads. If you do not, AdMob will serve only contextual
advertising. You can change this decision at any time in iOS Settings →
Privacy & Security → Tracking → Boxerium.

## Children

Boxerium is rated 4+ and is not directed at children under 13. It does not
knowingly collect personal information from children. The advertisements
served by AdMob are configured for general audiences.

## Your choices

- **Remove ads** — subscribe to "Remove Ads" inside the App's Settings screen
  to stop banner advertisements entirely (and stop AdMob from collecting
  ad-related data). Manage or cancel any time via iOS Settings → Apple ID →
  Subscriptions.
- **Reset local data** — delete the App from your device. All locally stored
  workouts and settings are removed.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the
top of this page will reflect the most recent change. Material changes will
be announced in an in-app notice on next launch.
